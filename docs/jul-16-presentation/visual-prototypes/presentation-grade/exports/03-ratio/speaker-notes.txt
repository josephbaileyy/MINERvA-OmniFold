BUILD 1
Speaker move: Show data and simulation occupying the same reconstructed feature space.
Purpose: Prevents the classifier from being introduced as an abstract ML object.

BUILD 2
Speaker move: Ask only where data are more or less dense than simulation.
Purpose: Reframes classification as a local density-comparison task.

BUILD 3
Speaker move: Convert the classifier probability into a likelihood-ratio weight.
Purpose: Provides the one equation worth retaining and explains what the output means physically.

BUILD 4
Speaker move: Apply the weights: simulation density morphs toward data density.
Purpose: Closes the loop visually before the audience sees the two-level OmniFold algorithm.
